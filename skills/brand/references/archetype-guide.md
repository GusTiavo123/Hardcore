# Archetype Guide — 12 Jungian Brand Archetypes

Reference consumed by Strategy (primary), Verbal, Visual, and Logo. Defines each archetype with voice defaults, typography associations, palette family, positioning pattern, and sentiment compatibility.

---

## 1. The 12 Archetypes — Core Table

| Archetype | Core desire | Strategy | Typical market fit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Innocent** | Happiness, simplicity | Wholesomeness | Personal wellness, family, meditation |
| **Sage** | Truth, wisdom | Expertise, guidance | Education, research, consulting, analytics |
| **Explorer** | Freedom, discovery | Adventure, frontier | Travel, outdoor, frontier tech |
| **Outlaw** | Revolution, disruption | Rule-breaking | Rebellious/challenger brands |
| **Magician** | Transformation, vision | Make dreams real | Visionary tech, deep transformation |
| **Hero** | Courage, mastery | Bold solutions | Fitness, productivity, challenge-based |
| **Lover** | Intimacy, passion | Beauty, connection | Lifestyle, luxury, relationships |
| **Jester** | Joy, fun | Levity, play | Entertainment, casual consumer |
| **Everyman** | Belonging, connection | Relatable, down-to-earth | Mass consumer, accessible |
| **Caregiver** | Service, protection | Nurture, support | Healthcare, nonprofit, kids |
| **Ruler** | Control, order | Authority, premium | Enterprise, luxury, financial |
| **Creator** | Self-expression, innovation | Build, craft | Design tools, creative platforms |

## 3. Do/Don't Examples per Archetype

Concrete copy samples that Strategy and Verbal reference when producing per-brand copy. These are templates — Strategy customizes them to the specific brand during generation.

### Sage
- ✓ "Cortás 40 horas de trabajo semanal. No 'mucho tiempo', no 'rápidamente'. 40 horas."
- ✓ "La evidence sugiere tres opciones. Te explico cada una."
- ✗ "Revolucionamos la industria con AI de próxima generación."
- ✗ "El mejor producto del mercado (punto)."

**Context**: documentation, legal copy, analytical content, technical explanations, consulting reports.

### Ruler
- ✓ "Built for teams that set the standard."
- ✓ "We've served Fortune 500 compliance teams since 2018."
- ✗ "Join our amazing community of builders!"
- ✗ "Anyone can achieve X with us."

**Context**: enterprise landing, premium positioning, financial services, executive-targeted content.

### Hero
- ✓ "Stop tracking habits. Start building them."
- ✓ "10,000 steps isn't a goal. It's today's floor."
- ✗ "Our app helps you explore your wellness journey."
- ✗ "Consider the possibility of a healthier lifestyle."

**Context**: fitness/productivity apps, challenge-based products, transformation-focused brands.

### Creator
- ✓ "You don't find the right font. You build the voice with it."
- ✓ "Every canvas is empty until it isn't."
- ✗ "Use our templates for easy results."
- ✗ "Design without thinking."

**Context**: design tools, creative platforms, maker communities, dev tools with craft positioning.

### Jester
- ✓ "Your spreadsheet is on fire. 🔥 We have the water."
- ✓ "Yes, we're named after a vegetable. Move on."
- ✗ "Our enterprise solution optimizes workflow efficiency."
- ✗ "We value collaborative synergies."

**Context**: consumer apps with personality (Duolingo, Cleo), brands targeting Gen Z, subversive B2B.

### Outlaw
- ✓ "The old way is broken. We know. We made the new one."
- ✓ "Your bank doesn't want you here. Perfect."
- ✗ "We're committed to delivering value within existing frameworks."
- ✗ "Join the industry leaders."

**Context**: disruptor brands, fintech challengers, rebellious consumer brands, movements.

### Magician
- ✓ "What took 10 engineers now takes 1 prompt."
- ✓ "The future isn't coming. It's running in your terminal."
- ✗ "Our tool does X well."
- ✗ "Step-by-step improvement guaranteed."

**Context**: cutting-edge AI tools, transformational products, visionary brands.

### Everyman
- ✓ "You don't need a finance degree. You need 15 minutes."
- ✓ "Here's what we do, in plain English."
- ✗ "Leverage our proprietary algorithmic infrastructure."
- ✗ "Exclusive access for qualified prospects."

**Context**: mainstream consumer, accessible SaaS (Linear, Notion), broad-audience products.

### Caregiver
- ✓ "Start where you are. No judgment."
- ✓ "We'll walk through this with you."
- ✗ "Optimize your output with our tool."
- ✗ "Compete with the best."

**Context**: healthcare, mental health apps, parenting, support communities, accessibility tools.

### Innocent
- ✓ "Breathe in. Breathe out. That's it."
- ✓ "Three questions. Three minutes. Done."
- ✗ "Leverage our advanced mindfulness engine."
- ✗ "Complex journaling methodologies."

**Context**: meditation/wellness apps, simplicity-focused products, kids products, clean-slate rebranding.

### Explorer
- ✓ "We went off the map so you don't have to."
- ✓ "Built in the wild. Tested in the impossible."
- ✗ "Industry-standard approach."
- ✗ "Safe, reliable, predictable."

**Context**: travel/outdoor brands, frontier tech, adventurous consumer products.

### Lover
- ✓ "We pay attention to the details you notice."
- ✓ "Made for the way you actually feel, not the way you think you should."
- ✗ "Maximize your productivity."
- ✗ "Mass-market solution."

**Context**: beauty/lifestyle, relationship apps, luxury with emotional depth, journaling apps.

## 4. Voice × Register Matrix

How each archetype expresses under each register. `~incompatible` = avoid — flag `voice_register_archetype_tension: true` in Strategy output.

| Archetype | formal-professional | casual-friendly | playful-bold | expressive-raw |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sage | "Our analysis demonstrates..." | "Here's what the data shows..." | ~incompatible | ~incompatible |
| Ruler | "Our platform serves..." | Too informal | ~incompatible | ~incompatible |
| Hero | "Achieve peak performance" | "Get after it" | "Crush your goals 💪" | "This is the line. Cross it." |
| Creator | "Design considered..." | "Made with intention" | "Built weird, on purpose" | "We don't apologize for the aesthetic" |
| Jester | ~incompatible | "Yeah this is fun" | "BEEP BOOP 🤖" | "This is unhinged and we love it" |
| Everyman | "We help people with..." | "We're here for everyone" | "Just, like, a normal product" | "Fine, sometimes we're weird" |
| Caregiver | "We provide support for..." | "We've got you" | "We're here, grabbing your hand 🤝" | "You don't have to do this alone" |
| Innocent | "Our app simplifies..." | "Keep it simple" | "Three taps. Done. 🎉" | Generally stays sweet |
| Explorer | "Built for expeditions" | "Made for the trail" | "WE WENT WHERE?! 🌋" | "Untamed. Not for everyone." |
| Outlaw | ~compatible but dulls edge | "Screw the old way" | "Burn it down 🔥" | "The rules were bullshit." |
| Magician | "Transform your workflow" | "Change how you build" | "Abracadabra your stack ✨" | "Magic isn't real. Except this." |
| Lover | "Designed with care" | "Made with love" | "Fall in love with X 💕" | "You deserve this." |

## 5. Register Modulation Rules

Effect of each register on the archetype's default voice attribute set.

| Register | Effect on voice attributes |
|---|---|
| `formal-professional` | Remove "irónico, playful, visceral". Add "medido, credible". |
| `professional-warm` | Balance — keep personality, soften extremes. |
| `casual-friendly` | Allow "directo, sincero, humano". |
| `playful-bold` | Amplify playful elements. |
| `expressive-raw` | Allow "visceral, crudo, emocional". |

## 6. Archetype ↔ Sentiment Landscape Compatibility

Used by Strategy for archetype scoring and by Gate 0 (coherence) for market fit enforcement.

| Sentiment landscape | High fit (≥0.8) | Medium (0.4–0.7) | Low (<0.4) |
|---|---|---|---|
| `trust_heavy` | Sage, Ruler, Caregiver, Everyman | Hero (careful with arrogance), Magician (watch for hype) | Outlaw, Jester, Rebel |
| `disruption_ready` | Outlaw, Hero, Magician, Explorer, Creator | Jester | Ruler, Everyman |
| `saturation_neutral` | All | — | — |
| `low_trust_context` | Sage, Caregiver, Everyman | Innocent, Ruler (if trust issue isn't about gatekeeping) | Outlaw, Jester |
| `insufficient_data` | Use other dimensions only (ignore sentiment weight, renormalize) | | |

## 7. Palette Family per Archetype

Used by Visual System as seed for palette generation. See `skills/brand/visual/references/archetype-palette-seeds.md` for HSL ranges.

| Archetype | Palette family |
|---|---|
| Sage | Cool deep tones (navy, slate, forest). Muted, authoritative. |
| Ruler | Deep sophisticated (navy, burgundy, gold). High contrast, premium. |
| Hero | Bold saturated (red, orange, electric blue). High energy. |
| Creator | Vibrant expressive (multi-hue, often 3+ accents). Can lean warm or cool. |
| Jester | Vibrant, sometimes clashing on purpose. High saturation. |
| Everyman | Neutral with warm accent (beige, terracotta, earth). |
| Caregiver | Warm soft (peach, cream, muted rose). Comforting. |
| Innocent | Soft pastels, bright whites. Clean, simple. |
| Explorer | Earth + sky (khaki, terra, sky blue). Natural, grounded. |
| Outlaw | High contrast extremes (black + red, black + electric). |
| Magician | Deep purples, cosmic blues, iridescent accents. Mystical. |
| Lover | Warm romantic (rose, burgundy, cream). Sensual. |

## 8. Typography Era per Archetype

| Archetype | Typography era default | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Sage | Editorial-classic or Neutral-modern | Serif headings, clean sans body (Fraunces + Inter) |
| Ruler | Editorial-classic | Didone display, modern sans body |
| Hero | Neutral-modern | Strong sans, tight tracking |
| Creator | Expressive-contemporary | Display fonts with personality |
| Jester | Experimental | Display fonts, rounded/playful |
| Everyman | Neutral-modern | Humanist sans (Inter, Source Sans) |
| Caregiver | Neutral-modern | Rounded humanist |
| Innocent | Neutral-modern | Clean geometric |
| Explorer | Editorial-classic | Slab serif or strong sans |
| Outlaw | Expressive-contemporary | Condensed industrial, brutalist |
| Magician | Expressive-contemporary | Display serif with unusual details |
| Lover | Editorial-classic | Elegant serif, italic secondary |
